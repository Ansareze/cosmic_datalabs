Program Content & Structure

The program includes:

  • Structured 12-week ML roadmap with weekly learning modules
  • Weekly assignments and hands-on tasks
  • 6 real-world machine learning projects
  • GitHub portfolio building guidance
  • Beginner-friendly expert support
  • Internship completion certificate upon successful completion

Our curriculum covers:

  • Week 1-2: Excel, Statistics, and Python Basics
  • Week 3-4: Pandas and NumPy for data manipulation
  • Week 5-6: Data Visualization and Exploratory Data Analysis
  • Week 7-10: Machine Learning Fundamentals, Regression, Classification, and Clustering
  • Week 11-12: Capstone Projects

You'll learn Python programming along with essential ML libraries including Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, and Scikit-learn. You'll also work with Excel for data analysis and GitHub for portfolio building.

Certificate & Recognition

Yes! Upon successful completion of all assignments and projects, you will receive an Internship Completion Certificate from Cosmic DataLabs. The certificate is MSME-registered and can be added to your resume and LinkedIn profile.

To receive the certificate, you must:

  • Complete all weekly assignments
  • Submit all 6 machine learning projects
  • Build and maintain a GitHub portfolio
  • Maintain a minimum 70% completion rate

Our certificate demonstrates your practical ML skills through real projects. While it's not a university degree, it showcases your hands-on experience and portfolio, which are highly valued by employers in the tech industry.

Learning Format & Schedule

This is a fully online program. You can learn from anywhere at your own pace, though we recommend following the weekly schedule for the best experience.

We recommend dedicating 8-10 hours per week to complete assignments and projects. The program is designed to be flexible, so you can adjust based on your schedule.

The program is primarily self-paced with structured weekly content. However, we provide regular support through email and may organize optional Q&A sessions. All learning materials, assignments, and project guidelines are provided online.

You'll have access to all course materials for the duration of the 12-week program. After completion, you'll retain access to your projects and portfolio on GitHub.
